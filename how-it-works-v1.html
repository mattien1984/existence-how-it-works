Everything begins with a Time Block

A Time Block is a structured unit of time that captures both what you intended to do and how it was actually lived — what you did, when, where, who with, and how it was experienced.

Calendars record what was scheduled. Time Blocks record everything.

Time Block Visual

On a typical day, you create 15–20 Time Blocks. Each begins as a projection of what you intend to do — the ideal you. After it's lived, it becomes a record of what actually happened — the real you.

Every Time Block holds both.

Strung together across a day, they become a complete picture of how that time was created.

The Time Creation Cycle
Engineer → Record → Reveal

This cycle turns intention into structured time, lived time into data, and data into self-knowledge.

01 Engineer

Structure your time before it is experienced

Pillars

Define how you want to distribute your time across four core pillars — Work, Life, Health, and Sleep. This sets the foundation of your intention.

Creating Time Blocks

Each block defines:

  • When it occurs
  • What you plan to do
  • Where it will take place
  • Who it will be with

Based on your Time Block title, the system automatically organizes each block into the right pillar.

Tags

You build a catalog of tags across three dimensions:

  • Activities — what you do
  • People — who you spend your time with
  • Places — where your time is created
Efficiency Tools

Engineering your time is designed to be effortless.

Favorite your most-used blocks and place them in a single touch. Create recurring blocks to establish your Rhythm — the time you want to consistently create.

02 Record

Measure how your time was actually created

Update Reality

After time is experienced, you update each Time Block to reflect what actually happened:

  • When it actually happened
  • What you actually did
  • Who you were with
  • Where it happened
Quality Score

The Quality Score is a signal (0–10) of how your created time compared to your intention.

  • How closely that time matched what you intended
  • Did this time move you forward toward your goals
  • How it felt as it was experienced
Reflections & Media

Reflections capture what that time meant — what you learned, what stood out, or what you want to remember.

Photos or media memorialize the lived experience inside the system forever.

Both Sides

Each Time Block now contains both the projection and the reality: what you intended and what was actually created.

Over time, this becomes the truest record of you.

03 Reveal

Your time, transformed into understanding

As your record builds, Existence transforms this data into insights — helping you see how you live and what your time is creating.

Time Insights

Summaries transform your recorded time into clear overviews across a week, month, or year. They synthesize what you did, how your time was distributed, and how it was experienced.

Your Quality Score reflects how aligned your time was with your goals and values.

Time Explorer

A structured view of how your time was created. See how much of your time was accounted for, how it was distributed across pillars, and how closely it matched your intention.

Break time down by activities, people, and places. Filter to view specific dimensions.

Time Agent

Converse directly with your data through E, your AI time companion. Ask open-ended questions across your full record and get on-demand insight.

The more you use Existence, the more E learns — building a deeper understanding of how you create your time.
