Existence — Homepage Framework

Created: March 27, 2026  |  Status: Draft v1

The visual narration:
Hook → Problem → Solution (The Cycle) → Product → Transformation

Summary:
Part 1 - The Hook — "This is for you, the Optimizer"
Part 2 - The Problem — "Despite everything you do, there's still a gap"
Part 3 - The Solution — "The Time Creation Cycle closes it"
Part 4 - The Proof — "Here's what it looks like"
Part 5 - The Close — "Your days are shaping you. Shape them back."
Top-Level Goals:

1. Hook the right user — the Optimizer — immediately

2. Articulate the problem — Time Disconnect — in a way that feels personal and resonant

3. Introduce the solution — Time Creation and the Existence platform

4. Show enough of the product to prove craft and credibility

5. Build enough desire to "Apply to Join"

Reference: Oura, Superhuman, Linear, Whoop — dark premium aesthetic, identity-first positioning, exclusivity CTA
Key principle: Speak TO the Optimizer, not about them. They already track, plan, and optimize. They need a system that actually shows them what it all adds up to.
Relationship to How It Works: Homepage earns desire. How It Works earns understanding. Don't explain the full system here — just make them want to see it.

Open Questions

1 Do we have any real social proof yet (early users, advisors, press)? Frame 2 depends on this. If not, consider cutting it or using founder credibility.
2 Which product screen do we show in Frame 8? Options: Time Block card, daily timeline, Insights dashboard, or the pillar split view. Needs to be the single most impressive view.
3 Frame 4 (problem deepening) — keep as its own frame or merge into Frame 3? Depends on pacing when we prototype.
4 Hero visual — subtle grid animation, breathing gradient, or product-hint motion? Needs to set mood without stealing focus from the headline.
5 Do we want the cycle SVG on this page or a simplified version? The full cycle.svg is built for the How It Works deep dive.
6 "Apply to Join" — what happens after click? Typeform? Waitlist page? In-app onboarding? The CTA's credibility depends on the landing experience.
