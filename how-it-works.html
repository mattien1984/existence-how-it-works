How It Works

Existence is a Time Intelligence System that enables you to structure your time, record all aspects of how it is lived, and turn that record into insight that improves how you create your time.

Scroll

Everything begins
with a Time Block.

Time Blocks are structured
units of time.

They capture both what you intended to do and how it was actually lived — what you did, when, where, who with, and how it was experienced.

Every Time Block belongs to one of four pillars — Work, Life, Health, and Sleep.

You define how much of your time you want to dedicate to each. This is your intention.

On a typical day, you create 15–20 Time Blocks.

And in a typical week, you create over 100 Time Blocks.

By creating Time Blocks, you give structure to your time, turning intention into something visible and measurable.

Record

Measure how your time was actually created

Update what happened

After time is experienced, you update each Time Block to reflect what actually happened:

  • When it actually happened
  • What you actually did
  • Who you were with
  • Where it happened
Capture the experience

The Quality Score is a signal (0-10) of how your created time compared to your intention.

Reflections capture what that time meant. Photos and media memorialize the lived experience inside the system forever.

Both sides

Each Time Block now holds both the projection and the reality of that time.

What you intended and what was actually created — over time, this becomes the truest record of you.
