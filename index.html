At the center of Existence is a process called the Time Creation Cycle. It turns intention into structured time, lived time into data, and that data into self-knowledge.

THE SYSTEM
Your calendar shows what was scheduled.
Existence shows how your time was lived.

Existence builds on top of your calendar. It introduces Time Blocks — containers that capture what you planned, how it was actually lived, and how that impacted you.

On a typical day, you create 15–20 Time Blocks — accounting for all time, not just what was scheduled.

STEP 01
Engineer

To engineer your time is to give it structure before it is experienced. Each Time Block begins as a projection of what you intend to do.
